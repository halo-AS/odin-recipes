Description

These crunchy baked chicken tenders are life changing! The secret to truly golden,
truly crunchy baked breaded chicken is to toast the breadcrumbs in the oven first.


Ingredients

  • Panko Bread Crumbs
  • Oil Spray
  • Seasoning
  • 1 Egg
  • 2 tbsp of Flour
  • 1 lbs of Chicken Tenderloin

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F
  2. Spread the Panko Bread Crumbs on a baking sheet and spray with oil
  3. Toast the Bread Crumbs for 3-5 minutes until golden brown
  4. Cut the Chicken Tenderloin into smaller chicken tender sized pieces
  5. Beat the egg and mix the seasoning in with the flour
  6. Place the chicken tenders in the egg and then lay on the flour
  7. Flip it over and then sprinkle the Bread Crumbs on both sides
  8. Spray a baking tray lightly with oil and cook the tenders for 15 minutes
