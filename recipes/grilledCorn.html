Grilled Corn

Home

Description

A traditional grilled corn recipe is served slathered with butter and sprinkled with salt. It goes perfect
next to grilled classic hamburgers, steak, grilled chicken or BBQ chicken. The pure taste of summer!


Ingredients

  • Corn (without husks)
  • Fresh Basil
  • Salt
  • Black Pepper
  • Stick of Butter

Steps

  1. Soften the butter
  2. Mix Fresh Basil, Salt, Black Pepper, and Softened Butter together
  3. Apply the seasoned butter onto the corn
  4. Heat up the grill
  5. Place the corn on the grill
  6. Turn to a new side every few minutes
  7. Repeat until the corn is steaming hot and tender
