Description

This is a great recipe for a night when you're feeling lazy - only
a few ingredients, nothing to chop, easy cleanup, and very yummy!


Ingredients

  • Cooking Spray
  • 4 Flounder Fillets
  • Seasoning
  • 1/4 Cup of Melted Butter
  • 3 tbsp of Seasoned Bread Crumbs

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 325 degrees F
  2. Line a baking sheet with aluminum foil and coat with cooking spray
  3. Rinse the flounder fillets and pat dry
  4. Season the fillets
  5. Pour the butter over the fillets and cover with the seasoned bread crumbs
  6. Bake the flounder in the oven for 25-30 minutes
